Neon Drifter 2049

Endless synthwave survival racing through a collapsing network. Hold the lanes, manage your form, and keep the Glitch Wall from swallowing the run.

Run Goal

Survive as long as possible. Score climbs forever until integrity reaches zero or the wall catches you.

Difficulty ramps in phases. Every 15 seconds the whole stream gets faster, and every 1000 score the spawn table escalates.

Forms

Switch between Circle, Triangle, and Square.

Only matching shape cores restore integrity and calm the wall. Wrong form means a mismatch penalty.

Controls
